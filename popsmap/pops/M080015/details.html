It is easy to miss this residential plaza at the northeast corner of Third Avenue and East 95th Street, even though the red brick paving, several planters, and trees and shrubs mark the spot. Ledge seating is available. At a recent site visit, no plaque was visible. Photo: Kayden et al. (2000)
