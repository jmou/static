This strip of plaza clamps around most of the street frontage of the building, on East 70th Street, the east side of First Avenue, and East 71st Street. Uses of the plaza include the common semicircular drop-off driveway, on East 71st Street, and extra sidewalk, with concrete planters, on both First Avenue and East 70th Street.
